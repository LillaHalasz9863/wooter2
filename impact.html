For each one purchased we ‘ll plant a bamboo! Bamboo fibre is a fast growing, sustainable resource. Wooter is official partner with the National Forest Foundation (NFF), a tree planting charity.

How do we help…

Our solution is to provide them jobs as bottle makers for our company which will help them earn more money to build wells for catching water from which they can make drinking water as well.
